The Process
HOW IT WORKS
01

You reach out

Message via WhatsApp. Tell me where you are, what you're working towards, and when you want to compete. We'll figure out if we're a good fit.

02

Onboarding and baseline

You fill out a detailed intake covering your current stats, training history, diet, and lifestyle. I build your plan from the ground up around your specific starting point.

03

Weekly review cycle

Every week you submit weight, photos, and biofeedback through the app. I review, adjust, and have your updated plan ready for the start of every new week.

04

Ongoing support

You're not left to figure it out between check-ins. Mid-week support, group community, and increased contact in the final weeks of prep when it matters most.

05

Show day and beyond

Peak week management, show day logistics, and a structured post-show reverse diet protocol. The work doesn't stop when you step off stage.
